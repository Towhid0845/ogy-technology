Hungry Guest Apps Platform for Restaurant Reservation in Off-peak Hour

The Hungry Guest is a platform that offers online restaurant reservations in Canada. It allows users to book and dine at top restaurants in various cities at the best price. Users can get up to 10-20% off every restaurant, every day, by choosing from different time slots and cuisines. The Hungry Guest also provides users with reviews, ratings, photos, and menus of the restaurants

Customers Benefit from HungryGuest are
  • Customer can save money and enjoy discounts of up to 10-20% off every restaurant, every day, by choosing from different time slots and cuisines.
  • Customer can book and dine at top restaurants in various cities with ease and convenience, as HungryGuest provides them with reviews, ratings, photos, and menus of the restaurants, as well as filters and categories to help them find their preferred dining options.
  • Customer can earn points and rewards for every reservation they make, which can be redeemed for vouchers and gifts.
  • Customer can share their dining experiences with their friends and family, as HungryGuest has a social media integration that lets them post their photos and reviews on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter.
Restaurants Benefit from HungryGuest are
  • Restaurants can increase their revenue and occupancy, by attracting more customers during off-peak hours and reducing food waste. HungryGuest helps restaurants fill their empty tables with hungry diners who are looking for discounts and variety.
  • Restaurants can enhance their brand image and reputation, by showcasing their menus and services on HungryGuest website and app, as well as receiving positive reviews and ratings from satisfied customers. HungryGuest helps restaurants reach a wider and more diverse audience.
  • Restaurants can optimize their yield management and operational efficiency, by adjusting their prices and availability according to the demand and supply of the market. HungryGuest helps restaurants use data and analytics to make informed decisions and improve their performance.
  • Restaurants can join a network of all the restaurants in CANADA, who are partnering with HungryGuest to offer online reservations and discounts to their customers. HungryGuest helps restaurants connect with other industry players and learn from their best practices.
