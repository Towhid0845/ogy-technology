About OGY Technologies

OGY Technologies Ltd is a on demand service delivery company based out of Bangladesh, OGY Technologies was inspired by the thought of providing a complete on demand service delivery such as B2B service, on demand food delivery service, on demand home maintenance service , on demand health consultation Service , digital marketing and Mobile apps development.

Main objective of OGY Technologies is to addressed and solve daily problems/demand of citizens and provide one stop single window on demand services for citizens.
